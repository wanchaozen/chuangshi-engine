# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free project.

Be constructive, discuss technical decisions with evidence, respect different
experience levels, and avoid publishing another person's private information.
Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, and deliberate disruption are
not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and community
interactions that violate these expectations. Serious concerns may be reported
privately through GitHub.
